Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gata Azul Tattoo Studio handles personal information collected through booking requests, store inquiries, and email communications.

1. Data We Collect

Depending on your request, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Shipping city/country for print orders
  • Order notes or booking notes you provide voluntarily

2. How We Use Your Data

We use personal data to:

  • Respond to booking and order requests
  • Confirm appointments and purchases
  • Coordinate shipping and customer support
  • Handle legal or accounting obligations

3. Legal Basis

Data is processed on the basis of your request for services, your consent when you send information, and legitimate business interests such as managing appointments and orders.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared only with service providers required to operate the business (for example, email and hosting providers) or when required by law.

5. Data Retention

Personal information is retained only as long as necessary for appointments, orders, accounting records, and legal compliance.

6. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data. No online method is fully risk-free, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data, or object to processing where applicable. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. International Transfers

If service providers process data outside your country, we use reasonable safeguards consistent with applicable law.

9. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. The current version and effective date are published on this page.

10. Contact
